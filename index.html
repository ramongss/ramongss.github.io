> Hello, World! 👋

I am Ramon G. da Silva, PhD in Computational Intelligence by the Industrial and Systems Engineering Graduate Program - PUCPR 🎓, and Data Scientist at Banco Bradesco.

I’ve been researching on applications of machine learning and artificial intelligence approaches to time series forecasting 📈. Some applications of forecasting in diverse datasets, such as: Wind energy of a turbine in a wind farm in Brazil; COVID-19 cases in Brazil and USA context coupled with meteorological variables; and Cryptocurrecy (Bitcoin) price over time.

I am a #rstats user, currently learning more about Python 🐍. I’m also an amateur guitarist 🎸 who recorded some rock singles on Spotify (check it out). You can find me on Twitter ✉ (or any other social account) and perhaps we can curse the latest Star Wars trilogy together 😅 (still sad about that).
